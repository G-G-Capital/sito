About Us

Our story

G&G Capital, is an American multinational conglomerate, based in New York. It was founded by Andrea Garavaldi and Gurpreet Singh in 2019 offering IT services, with the flagship company G&G Limited. The president is Andrea Garavaldi (CEO) flanked by Gurpreet Singh (CO-CEO). In 2020, the company had a great success thanks to innovative ideas with easily accessible costs. In mid-2020 the group expanded to become the world leader in most sectors, collaborating and subsequently acquiring world-class companies.
